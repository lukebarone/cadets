BC Mainland Division Training and AGM Weekend Kitlist

The kitlist provided is the bare minimum. Please pack everything on the list, depending on your group. The hotel will require a valid Credit Card and photo ID for whomever is paying for the room.

Back to homepage

The hotel provides the following in their rooms:

  • Towels
  • Face clothes
  • Bedding (pillows and blankets included)
  • Meals (attendees only - Saturday all day and Sunday Breakfast)

Everything else will need to be provided by you.

All attendees should bring the following:

  • Personal Toiletries
  • Casual wear (Friday evening)
  • Sleeping clothes
  • Notebook and pen, or a Laptop computer

Branch Member or Executive

  • Business-casual clothes (Saturday and Sunday)
  • Business-formal dinnerwear (i.e. suit and tie) (Saturday Evening Awards Dinner)

Navy League Cadet Officer

  • No 3 Uniform (Short-sleeve white shirt; black trousers or skirt; belt; black oxfords or parade boots) (Saturday and Sunday)
  • No 1 Uniform/Mess Kit (with medals) or Business-formal dinnerwear (Saturday Evening Awards Dinner)
  • NOTE: You do not need to bring your head dress

CIC Officer

  • "Salt and Peppers" Uniform (Short-sleeve white shirt; black trousers or skirt; belt; black oxfords or parade boots) (Saturday and Sunday)
  • Full Uniform/Mess Kit (with medals) or Business-formal dinnerwear (Saturday Evening Awards Dinner)
  • NOTE: You do not need to bring your head dress

Guest

  • Clothes for Saturday and Sunday
  • Business-formal dinnerwear (if attending Awards Dinner on Saturday night)
