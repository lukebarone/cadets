Hotel Information

Event Timing: October 13-15, 2023
Event Address has changed: The Eldorado, 3762 Lakeshore Road, Kelowna, BC.

All Hotel Room Bookings for your branch need to be booked by June 15th 2023. Any bookings after this date cannot be guaranteed at our price or availability.

For Room Bookings: online or telephone or email. Remember to quote "Navy League of Canada AGM" to get our room rates.

Optionally, BCMD can book your room(s) for your branch. This is over and above the registration fees.

  • This is a best-effort service. If you do not get an email or phone call within 72 hours of submitting your registration, reach out to us to check the status.
  • You will need a credit card and valid government-issued ID to check in. The reservations will be made under your name.
  • BCMD will hold the room(s) for you/your branch. No-shows will still be charged and are not refundable within 2 weeks of check-in time
  • The best effort is made to bunk partipants together (i.e. 2 males in a room with 2 Queen beds). If you are travelling with a partner, would like your own room, or another hotel request for your stay, please let us know in the Additional Comments above.
  • The costs are passed on to your Branch. Please get approval before making arrangements.
  • If you are willing, please indicate that you can share a room with another person attending. This will help Branches save on costs.
