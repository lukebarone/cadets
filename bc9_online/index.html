BC(9) Online Form

To be completed yearly, and when personnel change in the NLCC Corps or the Branch

The BC(9) Form is used to inform BCMD of any changes to the Branch or Navy League Cadet Corps (in terms of officers/instructors). Any time there is a change in Branch or NLCC instructors, this form must be completed.

Your @bcmainland.ca address will be assumed for the Branch President and the NLCC CO email.
